Purpose

Our lab investigates neurocognitive processes in numerical cognition by examining whether an arithmetic N400 component—a brain wave linked to semantic processing—exists across mathematical operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, fractions, and algebra to understand how the brain processes math.
