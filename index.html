Purpose

Our lab investigates the neurocognitive processes underlying numerical cognition. Specifically, we are examining whether an arithmetic N400 component (a brain wave associated with processing meaningful information) exists across various mathematical operations including addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, fractions, complex numbers, and algebraic equations. This research may help us better understand how the brain represents and processes mathematical information.
